## ðŸ“Š Methodology

1. **Snapshot Capture**: All predictions were generated on **January 3rd, 2026**, creating an immutable "time capsule" of AI foresight before the events actually transpired.
2. **Synthetic Intuition**: Models were prompted to provide not just a guess, but a reasoned strategic analysis and a self-calibrated confidence score.
3. **Calibration Tracking**: We measure not just *if* a model was right, but how well-calibrated its confidence was (e.g., do 90% confidence predictions succeed more often than 50% ones?).
4. **Independent Verification**: Status updates are performed based on verified global news and primary data sources.
